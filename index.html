SAVE THE PLANET

HELPING SMALL COMMUNITIES

ETCA brings together qualified engineers who are willing to provide consultation services to small businesses and communities who are interested in taking action against climate change, but need help understanding their particular situation.

PROMOTING SUSTAINABLE ENGINEERING

ETCA works to educate industry engineers and leaders about ways they can make their companies more planet-friendly. The organization encourages engineers to act on their responsibility to make the engineering industry a sustainable enterprise.

BUILDING A GLOBAL ENGINEERING COMMUNITY

By bringing together engineers from around the world, ETCA is creating a worldwide network of makers and innovators who are passionate about solving the problems that the Climate Crisis poses. ETCA is creating a community where engineers of all backgrounds and industries can volunteer their time and expertise to help save our planet.
